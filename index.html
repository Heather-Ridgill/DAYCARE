Welcome to your safe space

The purpose of Bedtime Bears is to help single parents or parents that work graveyard shifts and are sick and tired of begging their
family and friends to take care of the kids overnight, to be able to drop off their kids for the night without worry.

What we offer!

A Welcoming Environment

I know leaving the kids is one of the hardest things, us as parents have to face. I am a single Mom of 2 kids - so I get it. Starting from the time you drop them off to pick up, rest assurd all kids will be taken care of with the same love that I have for my own (because I have been in your shoes!) Every night will be another sleepover with their friends, they will want to come back again and again!

Hours

Regular Drop off: 6:30PM Pick up: 6:30AM Saturday Night-Thursday Night (My only nights off are Friday) * If you need special accomodations, and a slightly different schedule, I am able to work with you, just ask!*

Ages

Right now, I can not take anyone under 4 years old - and I do not have a limit of how old. :)

Certifications

I am Infant C.A.R.E CPR & First Aid Certified - Ask to see my certification card!

Schedule

6:30/7PM - Drop offs
7-7:30PM - Dinner/Snack Time
7:30-8PM - Play time
8-8:30PM - Brush teeth, get ready for bed, read
8:30-9PM - Night Time

6AM - Wake Up
6:30-7AM - Pick up

What I offer

1.) A safe space to play with new toys (and videogames, with your approval)
2.) A light dinner/snack with water or milk - if you have picky eaters, i totally get that, and you are free to pack what they love and I will prepare for them
3.) All kids will be spread out on the floor with a lot of blankets and cushions
4.) I also like to take fun photography of the kids, so would love to send you pictures and updates throughout the evening on how and what they are doing!

Please note: We do have 2 indoor/outdoor cats that all the kids LOVE, but if your little one has alergies, this might not the best fit.

Pricing

Pricing is only $30 a night! Do you have 2 kids? It will be $50 a night. Payments by cash or check are made every Friday. I will start accepting debit and credit payments soon! :)

Are you local to Fullerton and without a car?

If you are interested but dont have the transportation for your kids to my house - I can help! For an extra $3 per kid - I can pick up to take to my house - if you need help dropping off the next day please contact me. *Sorry - you need to provide booster/car seat if they need it.*
